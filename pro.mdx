Iris Pro takes the health scores and signals you see in the free plan and turns them into hard gates — blocking pushes, stopping builds, and surfacing workspace-wide findings you can act on. It adds workspace and folder analysis, git and build hook enforcement, dependency CVE audits, custom configuration weights, Config Studio, unlimited trend history, the full CLI suite, and Account Insights. Every new account includes a **14-day free trial** with full Pro access and no credit card required upfront.

## What's Included in Pro

Pro includes everything in the Free plan, plus:

- **Workspace analysis** — scan your entire project at once and get aggregated findings across every file
- **Folder analysis** — scoped scans for any directory in your project
- **Gate Preview drilldown** — see exactly which files fail each preset, with scores and warning pills per file
- **Git hook enforcement** — block pushes when any file falls below your minimum health score
- **Build hook enforcement** — block builds below your health threshold before review starts
- **Dependents table** — version audit and advisory scan for all project dependencies
- **Issues tab** — blocking issues and warning-level findings across the latest scan scope, with blocker and warning filters
- **TODOs tab** — all TODO, FIXME, and HACK items aggregated across your project
- **Clickable jump-to-line** on all findings — click any result to land on the exact source line
- **Custom `.irisconfig.json`** — override individual thresholds, detections, and severity per rule
- **Custom enforcement limits** — block on secret count, complexity ceiling, file size, or smell density
- **Custom scoring weights** — control how many points each finding type deducts via `healthScoreWeights`
- **Config Studio** — full visual editor for gate limits, scoring weights, and global default sync
- **Unlimited trend history** — track health score changes over time, week over week and month over month
- **Full Pro CLI suite** — `iris gate` for CI pipelines, `iris deps` for dependency and CVE audits, `iris todos` for TODO aggregation, `iris hook install` and `iris hook uninstall`, and `iris check` for directory and staged file scans
- **Account Insights** — enforcement activity dashboard showing hook pass/fail counts, push-blocked history, and daily usage

## Free Trial

Every new account automatically starts a 14-day Pro trial. You get full access to everything listed above from the moment you sign in — no credit card needed upfront. After 14 days, the account reverts to the Free plan unless you subscribe.

## How to Upgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the pricing page">
    Visit [iriscode.co/pricing](https://iriscode.co/pricing). Pricing adjusts for your region automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start your free trial">
    Click **Start free trial**. Sign in with Google or GitHub to activate your account. Pro features unlock instantly after sign-in — no waiting for a confirmation email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Before committing to a subscription, install a git or build hook and trigger a few blocked pushes or builds during your trial. Then check your Insights dashboard to see pass/fail counts and the health score delta since Pro activated — it gives you a concrete picture of what enforcement is catching.
</Tip>

## Sign In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Run the sign-in command">
    Open the VS Code command palette (`Ctrl+Shift+P` / `Cmd+Shift+P`) and run **Iris: Sign In**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with Google or GitHub">
    Your browser opens automatically. Sign in with your Google or GitHub account — no separate Iris password to create or remember.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to VS Code">
    After signing in, you are redirected back to VS Code automatically. No copy-pasting of tokens or codes required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pro features activate">
    Pro features unlock instantly. The sidebar updates immediately — workspace scan, enforcement hooks, and all Pro tabs become available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The extension never stores your password or any sensitive credentials. Only a license key is saved, in VS Code's encrypted secret storage.
</Note>

## Managing Your Subscription

Visit [iriscode.co/account/billing](https://iriscode.co/account/billing) to manage your plan:

- View your current plan and renewal date
- Choose card auto-renew or a one-time bank transfer checkout
- Cancel or reactivate a card subscription
- Update your payment method if a saved card expires

If you use card auto-renew, you can cancel at any time and retain Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

<Note>
  If you are billed in Nigeria, Iris can open a Paystack bank-transfer checkout for manual renewal. Paystack generates a transaction-specific account number you can pay from **OPay**, **Kuda**, **Moniepoint**, **PalmPay**, or your regular bank app. That account number typically expires after about 30 minutes. When you want to renew the following month, Iris opens a fresh transfer checkout with a new account number.
</Note>

## What Happens if Payment Fails

Paystack retries the charge automatically over a few days. Your Pro access stays active throughout the retry window. If all retries fail, your subscription is cancelled and your account reverts to the Free plan. Iris sends you an email notification if this happens so you can update your payment method and resubscribe.
